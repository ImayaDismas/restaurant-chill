Welcome

Imaya's is an open-air restaurant in the Maasai-Mara suburb of Narok, Kenya. Imaya's specialty is meat, and features an all-you-can-eat meat buffet. They serve a wide variety of meat and are famous for their game meat. It is a popular tourist destination
